About us

Who we are


We have made this website as a part of our experience of Vodaphone's 'Code Like a Girl', a 4-day programme with the aim to inspire girls who are interested in the coding industry. Perhaps one of the most important lessons which this invaluable opportunity has taught us is that the sky is the limit. Even though we were limited by constraints of time, we still managed to enjoy every second of developing our website, through pitfalls and merge problems (!).

Our website is inspired by Etta's love of learning languages and her experience with Spanish, as well as Kelisha's avid love of travelling. Combining this and our avid interest in coding led to the formation of this website.
